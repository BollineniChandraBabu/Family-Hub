A Story Worth Remembering

What they shared was real. From the moment they first met in August 2023 to the day they wed in November, Chandu and Sindhu moved through their love story with a rare kind of swiftness — drawn together as though the timeline itself felt certain. Their engagement came just six weeks after they met. Their wedding followed forty-three days later. A love that wasted no time.

Marriage is not a guarantee of permanence — it is a commitment to try, to grow together, to choose each other daily. Sometimes people try with everything they have and still discover that their truest paths lie apart. This is not failure. This is human life, lived honestly.

On 26 February 2026, the legal dissolution of the marriage was formally concluded. A document was signed. A chapter closed. But what cannot be dissolved is the shared humanity, the lessons carried forward, and the growth that happens inside every relationship — no matter how it ends.
