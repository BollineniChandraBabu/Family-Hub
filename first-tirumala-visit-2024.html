Our First Tirumala
Temple Journey

Chandu & Sindhu — Together for the First Time at the Abode of Srinivasa

📅   Friday, 16th February 2024  ·  Ratha Saptami

A journey that began with devotion and became a memory etched forever — climbing the sacred hills of Tirumala together on the blessed occasion of Ratha Saptami, witnessing the grand vahanams, and receiving the divine darshan of Lord Venkateswara side by side.

🌅 5:30 PM Departed from Tirupati (Sindhu's Hostel)
🏔️ 7:00 PM Arrived at Sacred Tirumala Hills
🏨 Ashiwini Stayed at Doctors' Quarters, Ashiwini Hospital
🙏 Ratha Saptami Festival day of 7 divine vahanams
Journey Timeline
✦
  • 🌆
    Thursday, 15 Feb 2024 · ~5:30 PM
    The Journey Begins
    You both set out from the old maternity hospital premises in Tirupati — Sindhu's hostel — hearts full of anticipation, beginning the uphill journey to Lord Venkateswara's abode. This single step marked the start of a lifelong shared memory.
  • 🌙
    Thursday Evening · ~7:00 PM
    Arrival at Sacred Tirumala
    You reached the seven sacred hills as the evening lights of Tirumala glowed in the distance. Checking in to the Ashiwini Hospital doctors' quarters, you rested and prepared your souls for the auspicious Ratha Saptami dawn ahead.
  • 🌄
    Friday, 16 Feb 2024 · Ratha Saptami Dawn
    The Festival Morning
    The sacred air of Ratha Saptami filled Tirumala with the sound of Suprabhatam and the fragrance of flowers. Dressed in devotion, you joined thousands of pilgrims to witness the grand divine processions — vahanam after vahanam through the day.
  • 🛕
    After the Sevas · Final Darshan
    Srivari Darshan — Together
    After witnessing the breathtaking rathas and vahanams, you stood before the sanctum of Lord Srinivasa for darshan — your first together. In that holy moment, with the sound of bells and the scent of camphor, a promise was silently made to return.
Memory Highlights
🪔
🛕 First Visit Together 💑 Chandu & Sindhu 📅 16 Feb 2024 🌸 Ratha Saptami 🏔️ Tirupati → Tirumala 🏨 Ashiwini Quarters

Among all the places one can visit together for the first time, Tirumala holds a unique reverence. The seven hills, the golden gopuram, the sound of the suprabhatam at dawn — all of it witnessed together for the very first time on the most blessed occasion of Ratha Saptami.

The Ratha Saptami vahanams — seven sacred processions from dawn to night — represent the seven sacred horses of the sun god's chariot. To witness all seven together, as a couple, is considered among the most auspicious blessings one can receive in a lifetime.

"On Ratha Saptami, Lord Venkateswara rides seven vahanas — but the greatest darshan is the one you witness with someone who matters. This was yours."
